# Feat Reference

> Compiled from Legendspring campaign rules and Pathfinder 1e core (d20pfsrd). All feats are presented with Legendspring campaign modifications where applicable.

---

## Improved Unarmed Strike (Combat) — CORE, GRANTED FREE
**Source:** [Core PF1e](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/improved-unarmed-strike-combat). Brawler 1 class feature.

**Prerequisites:** None.

**Benefit:** The character is considered armed even when unarmed — unarmed attacks do not provoke attacks of opportunity, and strikes can deal lethal or nonlethal damage at the character's choice. Unarmed strike damage for a Medium Brawler starts at 1d6 and scales with level.

**Normal:** Without this feat, unarmed strikes provoke attacks of opportunity and deal only nonlethal damage.

## Power Attack (Combat)
**Source:** [Core PF1e](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/power-attack-combat)

**Prerequisites:** Str 13, base attack bonus +1.

**Benefit:** You can choose to take a –1 penalty on all melee attack rolls and combat maneuver checks to gain a +2 bonus on all melee damage rolls. This bonus to damage is increased by half (+50%) if you are making an attack with a two-handed weapon, a one handed weapon using two hands, or a primary natural weapon that adds 1-1/2 times your Strength modifier on damage rolls. This bonus to damage is halved (–50%) if you are making an attack with an off-hand weapon or secondary natural weapon.

When your base attack bonus reaches +4, and every 4 points thereafter, the penalty increases by –1 and the bonus to damage increases by +2.

You must choose to use this feat before making an attack roll, and its effects last until your next turn. The bonus damage does not apply to touch attacks or effects that do not deal hit point damage.

## Catch Off-Guard (Combat) — CAMPAIGN REVISED
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc3)

**Prerequisites:** Base attack bonus +4.

**Benefit:** You take a –1 penalty for using an improvised melee weapon. Unarmed opponents are flat-footed to the first attack you make against them with an improvised melee weapon.

**Normal:** You take a –4 penalty on attack rolls made with an improvised weapon.

> **Campaign change:** BAB +4 is required (core PF1e has no BAB prereq). Cannot be taken at level 1 or 3.

## Improved Grapple (Combat) — CAMPAIGN REVISED
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc11)

**Prerequisites:** Dex 13, Improved Unarmed Strike.

**Benefit:** You do not provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a grapple combat maneuver, or any combat maneuver that can be performed during a grapple. In addition, you receive a +2 bonus on checks made to perform such maneuvers. You also receive a +2 bonus to your Combat Maneuver Class whenever an opponent uses such a maneuver on you.

**Normal:** You provoke an attack of opportunity when performing any offensive combat maneuver.

> **Campaign change:** Dex 13 prerequisite added (core PF1e only requires Improved Unarmed Strike).

## Improved Expertise Maneuvers (Combat) — CAMPAIGN CUSTOM
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc28)

**Prerequisites:** Int 13, Combat Expertise.

**Benefit:** You do not provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a disarm, trip, dirty trick, or similar combat maneuver. In addition, you receive a +2 bonus on checks made to perform such maneuvers. You also receive a +2 bonus to your Combat Maneuver Class whenever an opponent uses such a maneuver on you.

**Normal:** You provoke an attack of opportunity when performing any offensive combat maneuver.

> **Design note:** This feat bundles Improved Disarm, Improved Trip, and Improved Dirty Trick into a single feat, gated behind Combat Expertise + Int 13. Brawler's Cunning satisfies Int 13 — the only real gate is taking Combat Expertise.

## Improved Overpowering Maneuvers (Combat) — CAMPAIGN CUSTOM
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc29)

**Prerequisites:** Str 13, Power Attack, base attack bonus +1.

**Benefit:** You do not provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a bull rush, overrun, sunder, or similar combat maneuver. In addition, you receive a +2 bonus on checks made to perform such maneuvers. You also receive a +2 bonus to your Combat Maneuver Class whenever an opponent uses such a maneuver on you.

**Normal:** You provoke an attack of opportunity when performing any offensive combat maneuver.

> **Design note:** This feat bundles Improved Bull Rush, Improved Overrun, and Improved Sunder into a single feat. Gated behind Power Attack. Much easier to qualify for than Improved Expertise Maneuvers.

## Defensive Combat Training (Combat) — CAMPAIGN REVISED
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc7)

**Prerequisites:** None.

**Benefit:** You gain a +2 competence bonus to your defensive combat maneuverability bonus.

> **Campaign change:** Completely different from core PF1e (which replaces HD with BAB for CMD calculation). In Legendspring, this is a simple +2 DCM bonus with no prerequisites.

---

## Offensive Combat Training (Combat) — CAMPAIGN CUSTOM
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats)

**Prerequisites:** None.

**Benefit:** You gain a +2 competence bonus to your offensive combat maneuverability (OCM) bonus.

> **Why it matters:** The offensive twin of Defensive Combat Training — a flat +2 to *every* offensive maneuver (grapple, bull rush, trip, dirty trick) with no prerequisite. For a grapple/control build whose whole identity runs on OCM, this is arguably better than Weapon Focus (Unarmed Strike). It also unlocks **Improved Combat Training**.

## Combat Reflexes (Combat) — CAMPAIGN REVISED
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc5)

**Prerequisites:** None (campaign version).

**Benefit:** You may take a number of opportunity actions equal to either your Dexterity bonus or your Intelligence bonus, whichever is **lower**. If used to take an attack of opportunity, each attack is made at a cumulative -2 penalty to hit.

You may take a single opportunity action in a round where you are flat-footed.

> **Campaign change:** Uses **lower** of Dex or Int for # of AoOs, with cumulative -2 penalty per attack. Core PF1e uses Dex bonus with no penalty. As a Lizardfolk with -2 Int, investment in Intelligence is needed for frequent AoOs. Also: no feat prerequisites (core requires Dex 13 — not explicitly listed in campaign version, but the prereqs section is absent).

## Defensive Mobility (Combat) — CAMPAIGN CUSTOM
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc19)

**Prerequisites:** Dex 13, Defensive Combat Training.

**Benefit:** You may roll a defensive combat maneuver check when moving out or through a threatened square to avoid provoking an attack of opportunity from a single opponent.

> **Design note:** This is a new feat specific to the Legendspring OCM/DCM system. Unlike core PF1e's Mobility feat (which just gives +4 AC vs AoOs), this allows attempting a DCM check to negate the AoO entirely.

## Weapon Focus (Combat) — CORE
**Source:** [Core PF1e](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/weapon-focus-combat)

**Prerequisites:** Proficiency with selected weapon, base attack bonus +1.

**Benefit:** You gain a +1 bonus on all attack rolls you make using the selected weapon.

**Special:** You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take the feat, it applies to a new type of weapon. You may choose "unarmed strike" or "grapple" as your weapon for this feat.

---

## Combat Expertise (Combat) — CORE
**Source:** [Core PF1e](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/combat-expertise-combat)

**Prerequisites:** Int 13.

**Benefit:** You can choose to take a –1 penalty on melee attack rolls and combat maneuver checks to gain a +1 dodge bonus to your Armor Class. When your base attack bonus reaches +4, and every +4 thereafter, the penalty increases by –1 and the dodge bonus increases by +1. You can only choose to use this feat when you declare that you are making an attack or a full-attack action with a melee weapon. The effects of this feat last until your next turn.

> **Relevance:** Prerequisite for Improved Expertise Maneuvers. Brawler's Cunning satisfies Int 13 for qualifying.

## Unarmed Slam (Combat) — CAMPAIGN CUSTOM
**Source:** [Legendspring Campaign](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats#toc40)

**Prerequisites:** Improved Unarmed Strike, Base attack bonus +5, Improved Overpowering Maneuvers.

**Benefit:** You gain an unarmed slam attack (this is not a natural attack) whose damage dice is one step greater than your unarmed strike, and you add 1.5 your strength bonus to damage. You may not use this attack as part of a flurry.

> **Design note:** This is a separate FEAT from the Brawler's Slam CLASS FEATURE. For a Brawler, the class feature Brawler's Slam is superior (gained at level 3, scales with level, becomes standard action at 7). This feat is largely redundant for a Brawler, but could be useful for non-Brawler unarmed builds. The main difference: the feat version can't be used in a flurry, while the class feature can be iterated at higher levels.

## Additional Candidates (build sweep)

Feats surfaced by a later review that fit the build but aren't in the level 1–10 core plan. Pull them in at 11–15, via retraining, or (style/situational ones) via Martial Flexibility.

### Improved Combat Training (Combat) — CAMPAIGN CUSTOM
**Source:** [Legendspring](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats) · **Prereq:** Offensive Combat Training, Defensive Combat Training
**Benefit:** Roll a DCM check to then make an offensive combat maneuver without provoking an AoO (both rolls must succeed). +2 to your CMC.

### Brute Force (Combat) — CAMPAIGN CUSTOM
**Source:** [Legendspring](http://legendspring.wikidot.com/feats) · **Prereq:** None
**Benefit:** With a two-handed weapon — and **Brawler's Slam always counts as two-handed** — use 1½ × STR instead of STR + DEX on attack rolls. Makes Slams more accurate as STR climbs; does not affect flurry (unarmed strikes).

### Intimidating Prowess (Combat) — APG
**Source:** Advanced Player's Guide · **Prereq:** None
**Benefit:** Add your STR modifier to Intimidate checks in addition to your CHA modifier. Turns a dumped CHA into a strong Intimidate via high STR.

### Cornugon Smash (Combat) — APG
**Source:** Advanced Player's Guide · **Prereq:** Power Attack, Intimidate 6 ranks
**Benefit:** When you damage a foe with a Power Attack, make a free Intimidate check to demoralize (shaken: −2 to their attacks, saves, skills). With flurry + always-on Power Attack, this is per-round, party-wide debuffing.

> **Ranks timing (from the sheet formula):** Legendspring skill ranks = (1 if Expert, 0.5 if Proficient) × class level, summed across each class trained. Intimidate is an Expert Brawler skill, so its ranks = the Brawler level — "6 ranks" means **Brawler 6 (character level 7)**, or character level 6 if Intimidate is also tagged Expert in Barbarian at the dip.

### Rapid Grappler (Combat) — Ultimate Combat
**Source:** Ultimate Combat · **Prereq:** Dex 13, Improved Grapple, Greater Grapple, IUS, BAB +9
**Benefit:** After using Greater Grapple to maintain a grapple as a move action, spend a **swift action** to make another grapple check. Grapple → maintain + damage → second check in one turn.

### Improved Iron Will — Ultimate Magic
**Source:** Ultimate Magic · **Prereq:** Iron Will
**Benefit:** Once per day, reroll a failed Will save (you must keep the new result). Insurance for the build's weakest save.

### Snapping Turtle Style (Combat, Style) — Ultimate Combat
**Source:** Ultimate Combat · **Prereq:** Improved Unarmed Strike
**Benefit:** While in this style with at least one hand free, gain a +1 shield bonus to AC (the Style chain adds more). A hand is free between unarmed strikes, so it patches the build's low AC; a Brawler can flex it.
